Your Work

The lecture isn't only the past. The techniques on this site — magnet bends, databending, datamoshing, character rendering, the aesthetics of failure — are all things Willbear / Yaniv Schonfeld (github.com/willbearfruits) has built and shipped. Here they are, gathered, and pinned to the eras they belong to.

Each tool also appears inside its era page, in context. Live projects embed below; desktop apps and hardware firmware link to source.

Featured — live in your browser

WRONG §08 net.art · §10 databending · §11 datamosh

A browser that's wrong on purpose: it byte-flips images before the decoder sees them, pours combining diacritics into every character, and datamoshes video at the frame level. The direct contemporary heir to JODI — and a working anthology of half this site's techniques at once.

Character Glitch §06 ANSI/demoscene · §14 post-internet

Single-file canvas experiments where pixels are Unicode glyphs — physarum swarms, strange attractors, a hex heart in decay, and "the line", a sounded timeline of communication eras. The ASCII/ANSI tradition, pushed into generative art.
