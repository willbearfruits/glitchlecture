THE LAB

Creative Destruction Lab

Every technique in the lecture, in one room. Nothing here is a GIF or a filter preset — each demo is a real implementation of the method it names. Drag, sort, mosh, corrupt. They pause when off-screen so the page stays fast.

Webcam + audio demos start on a button click and run locally (127.0.0.1 is a secure context). Many accept your own image. Each card links to the era it comes from.

magnet TV§03 · Paik, 1965
Drag a magnetic field that warps and chroma-tears the picture, the way a real magnet bends an electron beam. → the magnet on the CRT
oscillons§02 · Laposky, 1950s
Lissajous figures on a glowing phosphor — drawing with pure signal. → electronic images
video feedback§04 · the Vasulkas
The image eating itself in an infinite tunnel. Add zoom + rotation, or point your webcam at its own screen. → video as signal
RGB shift§04 / §12 · channel split
Chromatic channel separation + slice displacement. The everyday "glitch filter" — and the one Menkman warned would domesticate the form. → domestication
real databending§10 · like HexGlitcher
Genuine JPEG byte corruption, decoded live. Switch operations, re-roll the seed, drop in your own image. → databending
pixel sort§10 · Asendorf, 2010
Contiguous runs of pixels inside a brightness band get sorted into smeared ribbons. Upload your own. → databending
datamosh§11 · Murata, 2005
Keyframes removed: blocks pull pixels along their motion vectors so one shot bleeds into the next. Feed it your webcam. → datamoshing
ASCII / character render§06 / §14 · characterglitch
Pixels become Unicode glyphs. Swap glyph sets, change cell size, turn on the webcam. → bedrooms & demoscene
cracktro§06 · the demoscene
Plasma, copper bars, a starfield and a sine-wave scroller — the demoscene's show-off virtuosity. → demoscene
plotter / algorithmic§02 / §05 · Molnár
Vera Molnár's "désordre": disorder rises down a pen-plotter grid as machine and chance share authorship. → art + engineering
AI melt§15 · synthetic failure
Latent-style flow-warp + posterise: the dreamy, doubling look of a model that lost the thread. Webcam optional. → AI glitch
net.art: a wrong page§08 · homage to JODI
Move and click. Glyph-storms, rogue buttons and source debris — the browser as composed malfunction. → net.art
glitch audio§09 · Oval / Cascone
Click play, then add bitcrush, speed-warps and random CD-skips. The aesthetics of failure, audible. → glitch music
zalgo text§00 / §01 · corruption
Combining-diacritic corruption rising over a phrase — the technique behind WRONG and characterglitch. → the manifesto
